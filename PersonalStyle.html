" Defining Fashion "

One thing that defines a girl is her style. Chic, bohemian, grunge, there are so many labels.. and yet, they don’t work for everyone. With fashion constantly surrounding us, it’s important to understand your personal style. Read below for 3 tips to discover your personal style.

 

" Find Your Undertone "

There are lots of tests you can take online to discover your undertone, AKA the shade beneath your skin. Undertones tell you whether you look better in cool colors or warm colors. Most beauty shops also offer free skin evaluations, stop by your local one!

 

" Look In Your Closet "

What colors do you notice a lot? Patterns? Styles? Everyone has styles they’re naturally drawn to, but knowing if the styles or colors you like go together requires more effort. Looking at a color pallet can do wonders.

 

" Go Shopping With A Friend "

When you go shopping at all, it’s easy to get stuck buying the wrong things. A dress that’s cute on the hanger doesn’t always look nice when tried on. And while it’s hard making choices just being in a store, bringing a friend along can help like crazy.
